Mike & Jai

By Karla Polo, Jeana Chesnik, Brandon Palmer & Seung Ho Jung

Verizon’s Open Innovation Lab and Parson School of Design had teamed up to host an AI design jam. Our concept was based on an A.I. Companion that helps shy children learn to overcome all their fears and insecurities. The direction our team took was to utilize projection mapping and machine learning as a foundational framework.

Why Are You New School?

By Karla Polo

This video explains why do I think that “I am New School.” I did it as part of the competition that every year The New School sponsors. The scholarship granted by The New School is only for Bachelor’s Degree, and after I had started, I realized that I had the possibility to apply directly to Master's Degree. Anyways, I decided to finish the video. It helped me to focus on what do I want for my future and gave me the strength to apply to Grad school at Parsons, The New School for Design.
