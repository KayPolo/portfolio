Electrokids Case

The Electrokids Case is a workshop where kids, using electronic components and recycled cardboard, will be able to build electrical toys. The objective I’m pursuing is to potentiate craft in kids and provide them tools that they can keep developing in their lives. Arts and crafts are fundamental elements of a child’s growing process; it exacerbates their creativity and unleashes their problem-solving capacities. Giving them access to knowledge that they do not necessarily acquire at the school expands their possibilities in unexpected ways.
