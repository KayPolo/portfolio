GIGBOX

The GIGBOX is a device that highlights the low wages of artists in the online music streaming industry within the gig economy. To interact with the GIGBOX, a user drops a coin in the slot and a timer start, indicating the duration of the play time, based on the local minimum wage.

Part of the exhibition "A More Fruitful Way to Spend Free Time." Plateforme Gallerie, Paris. May, 2017. Curatorship: Nicolas Maigret
