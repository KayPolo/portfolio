KEYSON

Many of us had experienced the frustration of going out and leaving our keys inside the house, being locked outside. I want to create a key hanger capable of alert you if you are leaving your keys behind when you go out. Extra Function: It will remind you to check if you closed your car doors.

This project was exhibited at the NYC Media lab annual event on 2017 at The New School.
