Know Your Customer (KYC)

Financial institutions are required to conduct background checks for all their prospects and existing clients. They have to verify the client's identity and assess if this business relationship represents a risk. This process is called Know Your Customer (KYC)

EXISTING PAIN POINTS

• Most financial institutions run KYC analysis manually.
• In large investment firms, a risk analyst can spend up to a month investigating a single institutional client.
• Existing solutions are expensive and rigid.

GOALS

Show our clients our problem-solving skills and domain expertise.
Leverage our in-house solutions and tech partnership capabilities.
Help the company to drive sells and engage new clients by showing instead of selling.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

How may we provide an out-of-the-box solution that streamlines the KYC process and that can be easily integrated to our clients existing systems?

PRIMARY USER

KYC Analyst
• Compile necessary documentation from the prospect client.
• Analyze several documents and publicly available sources related to the client.
• Detect potential risks and validate its accuracy.
• Generate risk report with supporting documentation

SECONDARY USERS

Institutional Client
• Request financial service.
• Provide required information and supporting documentation.

Account Manager
• Onboard prospect client and request KYC analysis.
• Follow up KYC process.
• Review final risk report and approve or reject prospect client.
