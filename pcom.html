Physical Computing

Here you will see a compilation of different projects in the area of physical computing.

KEYSON

Many of us had experienced the frustration of going out and leaving our keys inside the house, being locked outside. I want to create a key hanger capable of alert you if you are leaving your keys behind when you go out. Extra Function: It will remind you to check if you closed your car doors.

This project was exhibited at the NYC Media lab annual event on 2017 at The New School.

GIGBOX

The GIGBOX is a device that highlights the low wages of artists in the online music streaming industry within the gig economy. To interact with the GIGBOX, a user drops a coin in the slot and a timer start, indicating the duration of the play time, based on the local minimum wage.

Part of the exhibition "A More Fruitful Way to Spend Free Time." Plateforme Gallerie, Paris. May, 2017. Curatorship: Nicolas Maigret
